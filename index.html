Welcome to the 5th AGILEHAND Plenary Meeting!

  The 5th AGILEHAND Plenary Meeting will be held on December 2-6, 2024.

  IEEE ICIT 2025 is the 26th Annual International Conference on Industrial Technology focusing on the new ideas, research and works in progress within the fields of intelligent and computer control systems, robotics, factory communications and automation, flexible manufacturing, data acquisition and signal processing, vision systems, and power electronics.

  The ICIT conference is one of the flagship conferences of the IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IES). Its aim is to create a forum for scientists and engineers from around the world to share the newest research findings and ideas in the field of Industrial Electronics. The conference encourages professional interactions to advance science, technology, and fellowship.
