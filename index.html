PAPER SUBMISSION

The Joint International Conference of the 5th AGILEHAND Plenary Meeting & 2024 IEEE/CAA Journal of Automatica Sinica Conference (APM&JAS 2024) will be held on December 2-6, 2024.
It focuses on the new ideas, research and works in progress within the fields of Advanced Manufacturing, Robotics, Automation, and Information Processing.
Prospective authors are invited to electronically submit full regular papers of their work to Email: apmjas2024@gmail.com.
English is the working language of the conference. It is strongly recommended to use the set of templates in MS Word and LaTeX formats provided by IEEE. Please use them to create your paper (please do not modify the style or the format).

  Important Dates:

  •    Paper Submission Deadline:

    31 October 2024

  •    Notification of Acceptance:

    8 November 2024

  •    Final Paper Submission:

    20 November 2024

All accepted original papers, which must be presented at the conference, will be recommended to the IEEE/CAA Journal of Automatica Sinica (IEEE/CAA JAS) as Letters (3 pages) or to Journal of Advanced Computational Intelligence and Intelligent Informatics (JACIII) as full papers when passing peer review.

Venue, Hotel, and Transportation

Venue:

The APM&JAS Conference will be held at Tokyo University of Technology, 1404-1 Katakura, Hachioji, Tokyo, Japan (Google Map).

Hotel:

We recommend you to stay at Keio Plaza Hotel Hachioji (Google Map). It is located in the vicinity of JR Hachioji Station. Click here to book.

Transportation:

From Haneda Airport: Take Airport Limousine Bus (Timetable from Haneda-Airport to Hachioji)
From Narita Airport: Take Narita Express to Tokyo Station, transfer to Chuo Line to JR Hachioji Station
School Bus from JR Hachioji Station: Bus stop No. 4 at the rotary at South Exit of the JR Hachioji Station.
